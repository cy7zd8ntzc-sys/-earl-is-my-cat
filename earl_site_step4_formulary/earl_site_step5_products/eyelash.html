Eyelash growth

A plain-English guide from Dr. Auston Eckert — what may help with lash growth, and what's just mascara in disguise.

What's actually going on

Thin or sparse lashes can come down to genetics, age, or simply over-handling. A handful of treatments genuinely lengthen and thicken lashes over a couple of months — most everything else on the shelf just coats them temporarily.

What tends to help

  • Being gentle — skip the curling and tugging
  • Removing eye makeup without rubbing
  • A proven, prescription lash treatment used consistently
  • Giving it time — real change takes 8–16 weeks

What I'd skip

  • Harsh waterproof formulas and constant rubbing
  • Unproven 'growth serums' with no evidence
  • Expecting overnight length
