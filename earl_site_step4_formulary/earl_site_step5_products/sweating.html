Conditions/Sweating

Skin condition

Excessive sweating

A plain-English guide from Dr. Auston Eckert — when sweating is more than normal, and what can help manage it.

What's actually going on

Excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis) is when sweat glands are simply overactive — often the underarms, hands, feet, or face — beyond what's needed to cool you down. It's common, it's not about hygiene, and it's very treatable once you move past regular antiperspirant.

What tends to help

  • Clinical-strength options when the drugstore kind isn't enough
  • Prescription treatments for stubborn cases
  • Identifying your patterns and triggers
  • Knowing it's a recognized medical condition, not a quirk

What I'd skip

  • Assuming you just have to live with it
  • Layering on more deodorant and hoping
  • Embarrassment that keeps you from treating it
